About Polytechnic Courses

What is a Polytechnic Course?

A Polytechnic course is a diploma-level technical education program that focuses on practical skills and applied knowledge in various engineering and technology fields. These courses typically last 2-3 years and prepare students for technical jobs in industries.

  • Focus on hands-on, practical training
  • Shorter duration than degree programs (2-3 years)
  • Industry-oriented curriculum
  • Opportunities for direct employment after completion
  • Can be a pathway to engineering degrees
